Town

Annemarie Ní Churreáin & Rich Gilligan

First published in 2018 by The Salvage Press

Dublin city, referred to as Town by locals, is a landscape premised upon a complex history of siege, captivity and desire. In the Irish language, the city takes its meaning from the words 'dark pools'. This suite of poems Annemarie Ní Churreáin combines with the photography of Rich Gilligan to honour the pulse and flow of a population which despite an increasingly globalised atmosphere and within the context of an EU border, continues to bear forth the shadows and unique shadowlight of Dublin’s past.

The original, print editions of Town were published by The Salvage Press, the imprint of artist and designer Jamie Murphy. This digital edition was produced by the C21 Editions project, with the kind permission of the artists and publisher. It is edited by James O'Sullivan.
